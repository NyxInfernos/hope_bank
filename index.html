About the Organization

Who are we?

Noble People Society (NPS) is a registered NGO that undertakes social initiatives for both human and animal welfare. After the success of its Dog Lovers Portal, NPS identified another urgent social need: a digital platform to connect blood donors, recipients, and hospitals in real-time. This initiative aims to reduce the time lost during medical emergencies by making blood availability transparent, accessible, and coordinated.
